About

Echo is the first mobile app that will curate, personalize and guide you to women-centric media.  Anywhere, anytime.

Women make up the majority of media consumers and ticket-buyers. Echo will drive that purchasing power.

It will also be a signal-booster when it counts most for filmmakers: limited releases, opening weekends and premieres.

We believe Echo will energize audiences to incentivize the industry to work for all of us . . . while we're having fun!

Why women-centric?

There are many studies and statistics that explain the problems behind a lack of representation.  But if you're interested in hearing more women's voices, you likely already feel the need for it.  Echo is bringing about that change by being women-centric.

This means we focus on film, TV and VOD that:

Significantly features women in acting roles
Is created by, written by, directed by, and/or produced by women
Populates crew and behind-the-scenes positions with women


We also recognize that "women" is not a niche market with one point of view or set of interests.  Echo is proud to be intersectional in connecting you to women-centric media. This means recognizing that not every woman is white, middle-class, cis-gendered and/or able-bodied. Amplifying all women's stories is Echo's mission.

We also embrace allies of all kinds in order to bring as many women-centric voices to your screens as possible.


Our Company

Echo is a woman founded and led Social Purpose Corporation currently laying groundwork to become B-Certified. We're utilizing the power of business to solve a social problem while meeting higher standards of performance, transparency and accountability.

We are movie and TV lovers who are building Echo for engaged audiences and fans like you.  Not only do we want to see more of ourselves and a greater range of stories reflected on all kinds of screens, we want to impact the filmmaking industry — and ultimately influence distribution — to effect real change for both content creators and socially conscious consumers.
